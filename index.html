Before you start

  1. This is neither a satanistic ritual nor an ARG
  2. Please don't give me death threats when you are frustrated
  3. This game can be used to settle disputes, where higher score wins
  4. This game requires JavaScript and vanilla JS is used

Rules

  • Before you start the game, a random number between 111111111111 and 999999999999 will be autogenerated. This number will be obfuscated initially.
  • You have to use the slider to guess the number. You can see your choice in "Value" section. When you are confident that you have guessed the right number, click on Submit.
  • Upon submitting your choice, you will be provided with the Autogenerated number as "Goal" and a score depending how close your guess are to the Goal. You will get 5 chances, and at every chance your current score will be averaged with your previous score(s).
  • Your aim is to get "Dead On" on all 5 chances, by guessing the exact number as autogenerated one. Cumulative highest point offered in this game is 10,000.
